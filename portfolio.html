Dataset

SWAN-SF: analysis-ready solar flare data

A customisable dataset of over 1000 parameters derived from vector magnetic field data for active regions on the Sun. The goal is to make solar flare forecasting more reproducible and easier to use for downstream analysis.

Research themes

Three themes that keep coming back.

Flow measurements, simulations, and data products.

Flow measurements

LCT, helioseismic rotation, and HMI-based flow inference all sit in the same family: extracting a weak signal from difficult observations.

Simulations

Surface flux transport and related modeling let me test ideas, compare regimes, and separate observation effects from physical effects.

Data analysis

Filament detection, flare datasets, and survey-style products have taught me how to build things that other people can actually reuse.
